[date]7 October 2011[/date]
[title]Dr Rita Fan Hsu Lai-tai Lectures at The Chinese University of Hong Kong[/title]
Dr the Honourable Rita Fan Hsu Lai-tai, Member of the Standing Committee of the Eleventh National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, shared her views on 'Leadership: With the People, for the People' with some 1,400 students at an Assembly of Shaw College of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) today (7 October). 
The lecture is part of the general education programme of the Shaw College, which aims to enrich and broaden students' exposure as well as to encourage intellectual interaction between students and teachers across all the academic disciplines.  To strengthen its general education programme, the Shaw College has been inviting specialists of different professions to share their expertise and life experience with its students.  The Chief Executive, the Honourable Donald Tsang; former Chief Secretary for Administration, the Honourable Henry Tang Ying-yen; Financial Secretary, the Honourable John Tsang Chun-wah; Secretary for Justice, the Honourable Wong Yan-lung, SC; and former Convenor of the Executive Council, Dr the Honourable Leung Chun-ying, had shared their views on leadership in previous lectures.  Other speakers included Mr Dick Lee, former Commissioner of Police of HKSAR who lectured on ‘Smooth on the surface, firm at heart—The Art of Leadership' as well as Dr Lee Lok-sze who spoke on the issue of global warming.
