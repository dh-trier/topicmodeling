[title]Kunqu Lecture by Professor Pai Hsien-yung[/title]
Professor Pai Hsien-yung today (14 March) delivered a talk on "the Beauty of Kunqu" to a full house of audience at the Chiang Chen Studio Theatre on the campus of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).  Jointly organised by the Department of Chinese Culture and the Culture Promotion Committee, this event was also part of the celebration activities for PolyU's 75th Anniversary.  Professor Pai will deliver his second lecture on "Love in Kunqu Opera" at the Jockey Club Auditorium on 19 March Monday.
