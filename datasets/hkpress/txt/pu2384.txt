[title]PolyU DBA produces scholar-leaders[/title]
The Faculty of Business of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) hosted a reception on 26 October 2012 for its DBA (Doctor of Business Administration) graduates. The new breed of "scholar-leaders" will combine leadership, theory and high level of research skills to solve practical and complex business problems in the globalising world.PolyU President Professor Timothy W.
