[title]HKU Graduate School holds Award Presentation Ceremony[/title]
[date]12 Dec 2014[/date]
 
A total of 56 research postgraduate students of various disciplines received awards on December 9, 2014 (Tuesday) at the Award Presentation Ceremony of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) Graduate School. The recipients were selected based on the quality of their thesis and academic performance.
The award recipients included six students for the Li Ka Shing Prize, 10 students for the Awards for Outstanding Research Postgraduate Student, and 40 students for the University Postgraduate Fellowships.
In addition, 55 awardees had received other prizes, such as Hong Kong PhD Fellowships, the Doris Zimmern HKU-Cambridge Hughes Hall Scholarships, Fulbright-RGC Hong Kong Research Scholar Award Programme and the Three Minute Thesis Competition Awards were also invited to attend the ceremony.
