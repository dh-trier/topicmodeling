Citibank and the Faculty of Business and Economics of The University of Hong Kong (HKU) cordially invite you to attend the press launch of this unprecedented banking course targeting business and economics students of HKU on November 18, 2004.
Jointly organized by Citibank and HKU, the banking course is designed to provide students with a good level of understanding of modern banking operations and knowledge of essential banking practices. To give a broader curriculum, the course provides a platform for students to meet experienced banking professionals who will offer different perspectives by sharing their unique insights into banking.
Officiating guests attending the press conference will be: 
- Mr. Atul Malik, Country Business Manager, Citibank Global Consumer Group
- Professor Y.C. Richard Wong, Deputy Vice-Chancellor and Acting Dean of Business and Economics, The University of Hong Kong
More details of the course will be announced at the press conference:

Time: 3:30 p.m. (Media Registration: 3:15 p.m.)
