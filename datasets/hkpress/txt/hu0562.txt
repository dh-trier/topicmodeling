The University of Hong Kong (HKU) will hold a meet-the-media session for its new Deputy Vice-Chancellor tomorrow, June 11, 2004.Details are as follows:
Date: June 11 (Friday)
Time: 6:30pm
Venue: Room 223 (Kwan Fong Lecture Theatre), Knowles Building, the University of Hong Kong
At its meeting on April 29, 2003, the Council of HKU adopted the report, Fit for Purpose, produced by the review panel on university governance and management, comprising Professor John Niland, Professor Neil Rudenstine and the Hon Chief Justice Andrew Li. 
In implementation of its recommendation, the Council, at its meeting of December 17, 2003, set up a Search Committee to identify a suitable candidate to fill the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (DVC) position.
The recommended candidate will be appointed by the Council after the consultation with the Senate tomorrow.
For media enquiries, please contact Miss Polo Leung of HKU's External Relations Office at 2859 2600.
