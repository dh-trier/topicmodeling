
[date]2014.02.11[/date]
[title]PolyU announces the outcome of the review conducted by the Independent Panel of Inquiry[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) today announced that the review conducted by an Independent Panel of Inquiry (the Panel) set up in October 2012 concerning the issues identified in the Annual Report 2011/2012 submitted by the Internal Audit Unit has come to a close.
The University Management has accepted the findings presented and the recommendations made by the Panel. Upon careful consideration, the University has decided to terminate Vice President (International and Executive Education) Professor Tsui Lam Sin Lai Judy's employment with immediate effect. Professor Tsui joined PolyU in July 2002 and was appointed to the current post in March 2010.
Professor Timothy W. Tong, PolyU President, thanked the Panel for the effort in the review. He said the University is committed to the principle of good governance and is keen to uphold the highest possible ethical standards.
