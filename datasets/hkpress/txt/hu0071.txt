The Faculty of Architecture of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) will hold its graduation ceremony on Saturday, November 29, 2003.Details of the ceremony are as follows:
Date:November 29, 2003 (Saturday)
Time: 2:00pm
Venue: Queen Elizabeth Stadium, 18, Oi Kwan Road, Wanchai
Professor John Spinks, the Acting Vice-Chancellor, will preside over the ceremony while Professor K W Chau, Dean of the Faculty of Architecture, will address the assembly.A total of 146 higher degrees and 109 bachelor degrees will be awarded.
Reporters/photographers are cordially invited to attend the graduation ceremony.Please bring your name card or pass for identification at the entrance.For further enquiries, please contact the Faculty Office at 2859 2149.
