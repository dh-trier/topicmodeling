[title]PolyU to hold Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now inviting applications for its 2011/12 taught postgraduate programmes. To give prospective applicants first-hand information on the programmes, around 60 info seminars/programme consultation sessions will be organized by faculties and academic departments on the Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes to be held on 15 January 2011 (Saturday) from 2:00 to 6:00 p.m. on its campus.
The Info Day will give prospective applicants in-depth details of the programmes at the on-campus information seminars/programme consultation sessions organized by six Faculties namely Applied Science and Textiles, Business, Construction and Land Use, Engineering, Health and Social Sciences, Humanities, and the School of Design and School of Hotel and Tourism Management.
In the 2011/12 academic year, 75 taught postgraduate programmes will be on offer, including the following five new programmes:
Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Knowledge Management
Master of Science in Optometry
Master of Design (Urban Environments Design)
Master of Science / Postgraduate Diploma in International Hospitality Management#
Master of Science / Postgraduate Diploma in International Tourism and Convention Management#
# These two programmes will be offered subject to the Senate's approval.
For more details of the Info Day, please visit the website: www.polyu.edu.hk/as/tpginfoday. For details and application procedures of the programmes, please visit the website: www.polyu.edu.hk/study. As all applications received will be considered on a rolling basis, applicants are advised to submit their applications as early as possible.
For more information, please call Enquiry Hotline: (852) 2333 0600.
