[date]24 Oct 2003[/date]

A lecture theatre at the University of Hong Kong (HKU) is to be named Kwan Fong Lecture Theatre in recognition of the generosity and support of Dr Katie Yang and Dr Maria Lee, founders of the Kwan Fong Charitable Foundation.
The Foundation has supported HKU's Department of Music on promoting Chinese music and opera since 1998.A number of activities, such as workshops on Chinese opera, international conferences and musical productions, have been sponsored by the Foundation.A plaque in the lecture theatre on the 2/F of Knowles Building will commemorate the Foundation's continuing support to HKU.
Dr Katie Yang and Dr Maria Lee, Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, Vice-Chancellor and Professor C F Lee, Pro-Vice-Chancellor of HKU will officiate at the naming ceremony.Details of the ceremony are as follows:
Date:October 28, 2003 (Tuesday)
Time:11:00 am 
Venue: 1/F, Fung Ping Shan Building, the University Museum and Art Gallery, the University of Hong Kong , 94 Bonham Road, Hong Kong
The Kwan Fong Charitable Foundation, with several subsidiaries in Hong Kong and overseas, was founded by Dr Yang and Dr Lee in 1984.Its goal is to serve the community in meaningful ways.The Foundation has supported the founding of academic centres to promote East Asian culture and has also supported nursery homes for aged, physically-challenged and needy.
Members of the Press are cordially invited to attend. If you have any queries, please feel free to contact Ms Polo Leung at 2859 2600.
