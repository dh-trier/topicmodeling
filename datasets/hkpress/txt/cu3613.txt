 The 59th Congregation of The Chinese University of Hong Kong 
 
The Chinese University of Hong Kong held its 59th Congregation for 
 the Conferment of Degrees on Friday, 24 October 2003. At the ceremony, 
 Dr Edgar W K Cheng, Chairman of the Council, conferred 156 degrees of 
 Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery.
