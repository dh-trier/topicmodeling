The Faculty of Engineering of the University of Hong Kong will hold its graduation ceremony on Wednesday, November 26, 2003.Details of the ceremony are as follows:
Date: November 26, 2003 (Wednesday)
Time:10:30am
Venue: Concert Hall Hong Kong Cultural Centre
The Pro-Vice-Chancellor, Professor C.F. Lee, will preside over the ceremony while Mr C.D. Tam, MBE, JP, Chief Executive Officer of the Hong Kong Science &amp; Technology Parks Corporation, and Professor T.S. Ng, Dean of the Faculty of Engineering, will address the assembly.A total of 517 higher degrees and 538 bachelor degrees will be awarded.
Reporters and photographers are cordially invited to attend the graduation ceremony.Please bring your name card or pass for identification at the entrance.For further enquiries, please contact the Faculty of Engineering Office at 2859 2803.
