[title]HKU to send off over 650 Undergraduate Students to Exchange Programmes all over the World (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]10 Jun 2004[/date]
Â 
The HKU Worldwide Undergraduate Student Exchange Programme is an undergraduate student exchange scheme organized by the University of Hong Kong.This year, we will send more than 650 undergraduate students to more than 120 partner institutions in 20 countries including the mainland China for full-year or short-term exchange programmes.Most of these students will leave for their exchange institutions in late June and a send-off ceremony is scheduled for 11:00 am on Friday, June 11, 2004 at the Rayson Huang Theatre of HKU.
Before the Ceremony, a group photo of the officiating guest, Dr Alice Lam, Chairman of the University Grants Committee, Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, Vice-Chancellor of HKU, other honorable guests and the students participating in the Programme will be taken at 10:45 am at the Sun Yat-sen Place of HKU.Members of the press are cordially invited to cover the event including the group photo session.
For any enquiry, please contact Ms Polo Leung of HKU's External Relations Office at Tel: 2859-2600 or Miss Francisca Kwok of HKU's Academic Liaison Section at Tel: 2857-8613.
