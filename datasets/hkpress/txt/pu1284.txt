[title]Applications for SPEED part-time programmes now open[/title]
The School of Professional Education and Executive Development (SPEED) of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now inviting applications for its wide array of professionally run part-time programmes, leading to awards of PolyU-SPEED or overseas universities. An Info Day will be held on 4 June 2005 (Saturday) from 2:30 to 5:30 p.m. on PolyU campus, to help learners know more about SPEED's? part-time programmes.In the new admission intake, SPEED will offer a total of 15 award-bearing programmes at academic levels of Executive Diploma, Bachelor's and Master's. Among them, a new one-and-a-half-year programme named Executive Diploma in Human Resource Management (EDHRM) which is designed for aspiring HR executives to keep their competencies up to date, will be introduced, subject to University's approval, in September 2005. Other popular SPEED part-time programmes on offer in the coming August/September 2005 are:PolyU SPEED AwardsBA in Applied and Media ArtsBA in Marketing and Public RelationsHeriott-Watt University AwardsBachelor of Business AdministrationBA in Accountancy and FinanceBA in Business and AccountingBA in Business and FinanceBA in ManagementNorthumbria University AwardsBA (Hons) in Business ManagementBA (Hons) in Human Resource ManagementBA (Hons) in Logistics and Supply Chain ManagementBSc (Hons) in Applied ComputingMA in Human Resource Management and DevelopmentMA in Management StudiesMA in MarketingDr Dennis Chan, Acting Director of SPEED, said,"Continuous learning has become a part of our professional life. In additional to practical experiences gained from actual work, executives of various industries are now expected to acquire knowledge related to their jobs. Part-time programmes offered by SPEED as well as those by our overseas partners, provide a selection of high quality, useful and practical programmes for them. These programmes are exempted courses under the Non-local Higher and Professional Education (Regulation) Ordinance in Hong Kong. It is a matter of discretion for individual employers to recognise any qualification to which the courses may lead.For details of the Info day or copies of the programme brochures, please visit SPEED office located at Room QR802, 8/F, Core R, PolyU or its website at www.speed.polyu.edu.hk.Opening hours of SPEED are:Mondays to Fridays: 8:30 am -7:00 pmSaturdays: 9:00 am -12:00 pm (noon)Sundays and Public Holidays: ClosedEnquiry: 3400 2828
