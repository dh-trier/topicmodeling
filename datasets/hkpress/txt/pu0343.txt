[title]PolyU invites applications for its postgraduate programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now accepting applications for its 70 quality taught postgraduate programmes until 22 March 2004. 
The taught postgraduate programmes of PolyU are practical and professionally-oriented to enhance the competitiveness of students to suit the needs of industry, business and the professions. All programmes are run by its six flagship faculties ranging from engineering to business administration, fashion and design, social work and various healthcare studies. 
In academic year 2004-05, PolyU will offer brand new postgraduate programmes which are in line with the market needs. They include: five part-time self-financed programmes comprising Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Global Supply Chain Management, Master of Design, Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Construction Law and Dispute Resolution, Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Technology Management, and Master of Arts in School Guidance and Counselling; three full-time self-financed programmes comprising Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Facility Management, Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Fire and Safety Engineering, and Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Information Technology; and one part-time UGC-funded Master of Arts in Fashion and Textiles (Global Fashion Management).
The public can obtain application details simply by:
visiting the University's Website at http://www.polyu.edu.hk/admission 
writing to Admissions and Promotions Section, Academic Secretariat, PolyU, Hung Hom, attaching a 14" x 10" self-addressed envelope with postal stamps for HK$28
in person at the Admissions Application Form Distribution Counter, P202, PolyU. Opening hours of the counter are:
Mondays to Fridays: 9.00 a.m. - 7.00 p.m.
Saturdays: 9.00 a.m. - 12.00 noon
Sundays and Public Holidays: Closed
