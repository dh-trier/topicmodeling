[date]23 Nov 2004[/date]

The Faculty of Dentistry of The University of Hong Kong in conjunction with the Board of Governors of The Prince Philip Dental Hospital, will hold a Presentation Ceremony of Prizes and Awards on Thursday, November 25, 2004 at 5:30 p.m. in Meeting Room 601, the Sixth Floor, the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre, 1 Expo Drive, Wanchai, Hong Kong.
At the Ceremony jointly hosted by the Faculty and the Hospital, prizes and awards will be presented to about 150 dental students and ancillary trainees.
Among the presenters of the prizes and awards will be Dr. the Hon. Kwok Ka Ki, Legislative Councillor, Professor R.Y.C. Wong, Deputy Vice-Chancellor of the University of Hong Kong, Mr. Andrew K.Y. Leung, Chairman of the Hospital Board of Governors, Professor L.P. Samaranayake, Faculty Dean and Hospital Director, and Dr. E.K.K. Chan, President of the Hong Kong Dental Association.Many representatives from the University of Hong Kong and Hong Kong's dental community will be present.
You are cordially invited to send journalists to cover the above event.For further enquiries, please contact Ms. Polo Leung of the External Relations Office at 2859 2600
