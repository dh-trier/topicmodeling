[title]HKU to Launch Report on the Review of Governance and Management (Media Invitation)[/title]
[date]06 Feb 2003[/date]
 
An independent Review Panel of external experts appointed by The University of Hong Kong (“HKU”), comprising three eminent figures, namely Professor John Niland, former Vice-Chancellor and President of the University of New South Wales; Professor Neil Rudenstine, former President of Harvard University; and the Hon. Chief Justice Andrew Li of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, will hold a press conference on February 7, 2003, during which the Report on the Review of University Governance and Management will be presented to Dr. Victor Fung, Chairman of the Council of The University of Hong Kong, accompanied by Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, Vice-Chancellor. Details are as follows:-
Date:February 7, 2003 (Friday)
Time:2:30 p.m.
Venue:Room 9-02, 9/F Knowles Building, The University of Hong Kong
The Review Panel has drawn up recommendations that will better equip HKU to meet the challenges of the new century as it aspires to be a worldclass university capable of competing at the highest international level.
You are cordially invited to attend the press conference.Please note that the press conference will be conducted mainly in English. Questions in Cantonese will be welcome.For enquiries, please contact:
