[title]Sixteen HKU Staff receive Long Service Awards[/title]
[date]30 May 2007[/date]
 
A total of 16 senior staff of The University of Hong Kong (HKU) who have served HKU for over 25 years received long service awards in a ceremony today (May 30, 2007).
The annual award presentation ceremony held for honouring our dedicated academic and senior administrative staff was officiated by HKU Vice-Chancellor Professor Lap-Chee Tsui. Those being awarded in the 14th annual ceremony include teachers and professionals from various academic and support service departments. (Please refer to the list of recipients below.) 
For photos of today's event, please click the "Photo Gallery" icon at http://www.hku.hk/media/.
