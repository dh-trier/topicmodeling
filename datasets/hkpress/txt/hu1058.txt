[title]HKU and London Business School to enter into collaboration (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]07 Jan 2005[/date]
Â 
The University of Hong Kong (HKU) and the London Business School will hold a signing ceremony on a collaborative agreement on Monday, January 10, 2005 at the Mandarin Hotel at 3:00 p.m.
At the signing ceremony will be Professor Y.C. Richard Wong, Acting Dean of the Faculty of Business and Economics at HKU, and Professor Laura Tyson, Dean, London Business School. 
The new collaborative agreement will see London Business School host a class of up to 60 HKU MBA students per year for one term of learning in London from 2007 to study electives as part of their MBA course. The class will come from the new MBA programme to be offered by HKU.
After completing core courses at HKU, students will travel to London to study with MBA students of London Business School and participate in other activities that would further integrate them into the student life at the School.
Also attending the signing ceremony will be Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, Vice-Chancellor of HKU, and Dr Victor Fung, Chairman of the Council at HKU.Media representatives are welcome to cover the event.
