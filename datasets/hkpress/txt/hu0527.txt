[title]HKU holds Long Service Award Presentation Ceremony for senior staff members[/title]
[date]10 Jun 2015[/date]
 


The Long Service Award Presentation Ceremony of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) for academic and senior administrative staff members was held today (June 10, Wednesday) in Loke Yew Hall. The President and Vice-Chancellor Professor Peter Mathieson officiated at the ceremony to present awards to over 60 staff members to thank them for their dedication, commitment and loyalty to the University.
Nine members received the 35-year award, 23 members the 25-year award, and 33 members the 15-year award. The last award was introduced in 2014.
