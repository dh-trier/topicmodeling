[title]PolyU names classroom after Mr Clement Cheuk[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) has recently named a classroom at Wing BC after Mr Clement Cheuk to show its appreciation to Mr Clement Cheuk, Executive Director and Chairman of 3 Wells Watch Industries Group, and Mrs Corona Cheuk, Executive Director, for their generous donation to support the future development of PolyU.
Mr Patrick Poon (first from right), Council member and Treasurer of the University and Chairman of the University's Fund-raising Committee and Professor Angelina Yuen (first from left), Vice President (Institutional Advancement and Partnership) of PolyU, presented a souvenir to Mr Clement Cheuk (second from left) and Mrs Corona Cheuk (second from right).
