[title]PolyU ranked among the top 30 Asian universities[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) has moved up from the 38th place in 2009 to the 30th place in 2010, according to the latest Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) Asian University Rankings.  In the Subject Ranking by IT &amp; engineering, PolyU has progressed from the 33rd place to 24th place over the year.
The improvement in ranking has well demonstrated that PolyU's  pursuit of "Academic Excellence in a Professional Context" is well recognized among academia in the region.  Upholding the motto "To learn and to apply, for the benefit of mankind", PolyU will continue to nurture talents and professionals for the society, and foster academic and cultural exchange with different regions, particularly the Pearl River Delta area.  Committed to an application-oriented research culture, the University is keen to make use of the research achievements for the benefits of mankind.
