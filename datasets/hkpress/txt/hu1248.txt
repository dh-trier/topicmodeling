[title]2014 Asian Games gold medal winner HKU Arts student Hayley Chan back to HK[/title]
[date]03 Oct 2014[/date]
 


Arts Student of the University of Hong Kong Hayley Chan Hei-man was back to Hong Kong with her gold medal won in women's RS:X windsurfing at the 2014 Asian Games in Incheon, South Korea. Hayley had won in all twelve races of the event.
HKU President and Vice-Chancellor Professor Peter Mathieson met Hayley today (October 3) to congratulate her of her extraordinary achievement.
