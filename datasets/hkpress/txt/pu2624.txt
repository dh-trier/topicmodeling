[title]Professor Cheng Siwei speaks on Green Economy[/title]
Professor Cheng Siwei, economist and former Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, gave a talk on green economy at a luncheon organized by the Faculty of Business of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) today (16 October 2013).
PolyU President Professor Timothy W. Tong said PolyU is committed to linking education and research to the real world, and ‘sustainability’ is one of the strategic priorities of the University.
Professor Cheng Siwei suggested China to develop low carbon economy in four dimensions.
Professor Edwin Cheng (left), Dean of PolyU’s Faculty of Business, served as the moderator of Q &amp; A session.
