[title]PolyU partners with Chinese Olympic Committee in research and technology development for the 2008 Beijing Olympics[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) yesterday (10 May) signed an agreement with the Science and Education Department (SED) of the Chinese Olympic Committee (COC). Under the agreement, PolyU was designated by the SED of COC as a collaborative research partner for the 2008 Beijing Olympics and will send forth its experts from the Faculty of Health and Social Sciences (FHSS) to help prepare athletes for the Beijing Olympic Games.
The agreement was signed by Mr. Jiang Zhi-xue, Director, SED, COC and Director, Science and Education Department, State Sport General Administration of China (SSGAC) and Prof. Poon Chung-kwong, PolyU President. The signing ceremony was witnessed by Mr Chen En-tang, Director of Division of Science and Technology, SED, COC and Director, Division of Science and Technology of Science and Education Department, SSGAC, Prof. Cai You-zhi, Associate Professor and Associate Director of Research Office, Beijing Sport University, Prof. Ko Jan-ming, Vice President (Research Development) and Prof. Thomas K.S. Wong, Dean of FHSS.
