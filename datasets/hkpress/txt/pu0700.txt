[title]FinMan Chairman tours around Financial Services Career Exhibition[/title]
The following is issued on behalf of the Advisory Committee on Human Resources Development in the Financial Services Sector: The Chairman of the Advisory Committee on Human Resources Development in the Financial Services Sector (FinMan Committee), Mr Chan Tze-ching and the Dean of the Faculty of Business and Director of the Graduate School of Business of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Professor Judy Tsui, today (October 26) toured round the 'Financial Services Career Exhibition' in The Hong Kong Polytechnic University(PolyU). The three-day exhibition is jointly organised by the FinMan Committee and PolyU.Some 40 renowned financial services companies/organisations, including banks, insurance companies, accounting firms, investments institutions, financial regulators and professional bodies, take part in the career exhibition.The exhibition provides a precious opportunity for students to see for themselves the career opportunities in the best financial services companies/organisations.Â  Apart from exhibition booths, career talks and seminars are conducted individually by the companies/organisations.
