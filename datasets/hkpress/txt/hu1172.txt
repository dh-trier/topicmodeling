[title]HKU holds Information Day for Undergraduate Admissions 2014[/title]
[date]08 Nov 2014[/date]
 


The University of Hong Kong (HKU) held an Information Day for Undergraduate Admissions today (November 8).
An opening ceremony was held at University Courtyard of the Centennial Campus. Two admission talks were held at the Grand Hall, Lee Shau Kee Lecture Centre for f interested parents and students to have an understanding of the admission arrangements and requirements through JUPAS and non-JUPAS.
The Information Day aims to introduce HKU’s academic programmes and curriculum, student activities and campus facilities to the public, in particular secondary school students, teachers and parents. Information booths were set up by the Faculties and talks were held to introduce the wide variety of academic programmes. Teaching and learning facilities, teaching departments and some halls of residence were open to the public for a glimpse of the campus life.
For more information about the Information Day for Undergraduate Admissions 2014, please visit http://www.infoday.hku.hk/.
For more information about HKU admissions, please visit www.hku.hk/dse. 
More information day photos can be viewed and downloaded at this website later today: http://www4.hku.hk/photos/index.php/for_media_only/gallery.
