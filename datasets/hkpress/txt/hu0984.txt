[title]HKU Civil Engineering Students to Receive Scholarships (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]24 Jan 2005[/date]
Â 
Twenty-three students from the Department of Civil Engineering of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) are to receive scholarships at the ceremony held on Wednesday, January 26, 2005 at 5:15pm in the Eliot Room, 14/F, K.K. Leung Building, the University of Hong Kong. 
Souvenirs will be presented to all donors by the Vice-Chancellor Professor Lap-Chee Tsui at the ceremony for their generosity in offering contributions to the cause of academic excellence.Professor Y.K. Cheung, Special Advisor to the Vice-Chancellor, will address the assembly.
Reporters/photographers are cordially invited to attend.For further enquiries, please contact Miss Polo Leung of External Relations Office at 2859 2600.
