The Congregation of the University of Hong Kong will be held on Monday, December 1.Reporters and photographers are cordially invited to cover the event.Details as follows:
Date :December 1, 2003 (Monday)
Time :2:30pm
Venue :Hong Kong Coliseum, Hung Hom, Kowloon
The Pro-Chancellor of the University, Dr. the Hon. David K.P. Li, will preside over the Congregation and the Vice-Chancellor, Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, will address the assembly.This year 2,149 graduands will be awarded postgraduate degrees, including 193 at the doctoral level and 1,956 at the master level.3,036 graduands will receive bachelor degrees.
Reporters and photographers should arrive at the Coliseum 15 minutes before the start of the Congregation and go to the Reception Counter (Brown Gate) next to the Box Office.Please bring your name card or pass for identification at the entrance.While the Congregation is in progress, please refrain from leaving the Coliseum.Photographers are remind to bring their long lenses.For further enquiries, please contact Miss Polo Leung at HKU's External Relations Office at 2859 2600.
