[title]PolyU invites nominations for Sixth Outstanding Alumni Award[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) and the Federation of PolyU Alumni Associations (FHKPUAA) are jointly organizing the selection for "Outstanding PolyU Alumni Award" to accord public recognition to its distinguished graduates. Nominations for the selection are invited from today (25 September) until 12 January 2007.   Launched in late 1996, this biennial selection aims at honouring outstanding graduates of PolyU and its forerunners â the Government Trade School, the Hong Kong Technical College and the Hong Kong Polytechnic â  for their distinguished professional accomplishments and significant contributions to the community and their Alma Mater.  Over the years, PolyU and its forerunners have sent forth more than 240,000 graduates.  A total of 30 distinguished alumni in various professions have been honoured in the past award selections and they are fine testimony of PolyU's accomplishments in nurturing professional talents for the community. The seven-member Panel of Judges of this year's award, made up of senior government officials and renowned community and business leaders, is chaired by The Hon. Stephen Ip Shu-kwan, GBS, JP, Secretary for Economic Development and Labour of the HKSAR Government.  Its members, in alphabetical order, include: Mr Chan Tze-ching, JP, Citigroup Country Officer, Hong Kong and Head of Greater China, Corporate &amp; Investment Banking, Citigroup; The Hon. Henry Fan Hung-ling, SBS, JP, Member of Executive Council, HKSAR; The Hon. Vincent Fang Kang, JP, Member of Legislative Council and Chief Executive Officer, Toppy Co. (Hong Kong) Ltd.; Dr Lam Tai-fai, BBS, JP, First Vice-President, the Federation of PolyU Alumni Associations and Managing Director, Peninsula Knitters Ltd.; Dr Ng Tat-lun, BBS, JP, President, the Federation of PolyU Alumni Associations; and Prof. Poon Chung-kwong, GBS, JP, President, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.  All graduates of the Institution are eligible to be nominated.  Preliminary screening of the selection will be conducted by the Honorary Selection Consultant, Ernst &amp; Young Certified Public Accountants, in January 2007.  The Panel of Judges will then select the award winners based on the selection criteria relating to the candidates' personal achievements / accomplishments in his / her profession, their contributions to the local community in which he / she lives, and his / her support for PolyU and its alumni development.  Results of the selection will be publicly announced in February 2007.  At the Award Presentation Ceremony and Dinner scheduled for 12 April 2007 (Thursday), each winner will be presented an "Outstanding PolyU Alumni Award" trophy and proposers of the award winners will receive souvenirs in appreciation of their nominations. The nomination forms are now obtainable at the following venues on PolyU campus: - Alumni Affairs &amp; Development Office - Office of the Federation of PolyU Alumni Associations - Pao Yue-kong Library - Academic Secretariat Service Center- Book Shop  Soft copy of the nomination forms and other details on the award selection can also be downloaded from the website at https://www.polyu.edu.hk/aado/alumni/opaa2007.
