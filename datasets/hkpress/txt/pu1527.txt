[title]PolyU to offer a variety of summer activities[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) will be hosting a wide range of exciting and fun-filled educational activities for local secondary students. 
Speaking on the objectives of these summer activities, Mrs Agnes Kan, Assistant Academic Secretary, said, "PolyU runs a number of summer camps and workshops every year for JUPAS applicants' as well as secondary students who are keen to know more about our academic programmes. Moreover, the experience of staying in the Students Halls of Residence is also a wonderful chance to get a taste of university life."
Several summer camps and workshops that are tailor-made for secondary students to gain first-hand experience will be held at PolyU from June to August. These include:Â 
An innovative training camp named "Sparkles of Life Camp" will be organized for secondary students to experience rigorous training that helps nurture their self-esteem, social and leadership skills; The "PolyU Summer Business Camp 2006" will be hosted by the Faculty of Business to keep participants abreast of the current economic situation in Hong Kong;The "Design Summer Workshops 2006" will be staged by the School of Design to develop their creative thinking;"A Touch of Science &amp; Textiles Summer Camp" will also be organized by the Faculty of Applied Science and Textiles(Details are attached.)
Interested students, school teachers and parents can visit the following website: www.polyu.edu.hk/schoolcorner-activities or contact Ms Sharon Tse at 2766 5141 for the latest information and enrolment details. As places are limited, interested parties are advised to act fast before enrolment is closed.
