[title]NBA All-Star and Gold Medallist Kevin Durant visits PolyU[/title]
Kevin Durant, NBA All-Star and London 2012 Olympics Gold Medallist, met with some 200 participants of Nike All-Hong Kong Youth Basketball League today (30 August) at Shaw Sports Complex of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).
An intensive training session was organized during which Kevin Durant coached players on their basketball skills and shooting technique.
Before the clinic concluded, Kevin Durant shared some tips with the players and the audience on how they could improve their skills and emphasized the importance of training.
Professor Alex Wai, PolyU Vice President (Research Development), received a signed basketball from Kevin Durant on behalf of the university.
