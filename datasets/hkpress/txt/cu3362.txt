
[title]CUHK Fourth Honorary Fellowship Conferment Ceremony[/title]

 




[date]19 May 2005[/date]

The 
 Chinese University of Hong Kong 
 Fourth Honorary Fellowship Conferment Ceremony 
 
The Fourth Honorary Fellowship Conferment Ceremony of The Chinese 
 University of Hong Kong will take place at 5:00 pm on Monday, 23 
 May 2005 at Lee Hysan Concert Hall, Esther Lee Building on the campus. 
 Nine distinguished persons who are closely associated with CUHK 
 will be conferred Honorary Fellowship in recognition of their significant 
 contributions to the University and the community. They are: 
Dr 
 CHEUNG Huen Cheong, Thomas 
 
Dr CHOU Wen Hsien 
 
Mr LEE Kam Chung 
 
Mr LEE Woo Sing 
 
Mr LI Fook Hing 
 
Mr MONG Tak Yeung, David
 
Professor MUN Kin Chok
 
Dr WANG Cheung Tze, Charles
 
Ms YAN Hau Yee, Lina
