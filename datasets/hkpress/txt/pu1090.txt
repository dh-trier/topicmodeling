
[date]2008.12.31[/date]
[title]PolyU dedicates a tree to long-serving President[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) dedicated a tree on campus to retiring President Prof. Poon Chung-kwong during his last day of service (31 December) as a memento of his 18 years of distinguished service to the Institution.
Prof. Poon has made tremendous contributions to the advancement of higher education in the territory and towards the development of PolyU, particularly in leading the Institution to achieve full university status in 1994 during his tenure. The University Council has also agreed to confer the title of President Emeritus on Prof. Poon.
