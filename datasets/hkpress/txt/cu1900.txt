[title]CUHK Orientation Day for Undergraduate Admissions[/title]
The CUHK Orientation Day for Undergraduate Admissions will be held on 18 October 2014 (Saturday) from 8:30 am to 6:00 pm. Highlights of the event include:
Admission Talks
Faculty / Department Tours
Programme Consultations
Exhibitions, etc.
The event will be a good opportunity for secondary school students, teachers and parents to obtain useful information about CUHK and its undergraduate programmes. For further details, please visit http://www.cuhk.edu.hk/oday.
