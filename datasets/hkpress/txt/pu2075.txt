[title]PolyU community shows support to Japan[/title]
In addition to the campus-wide fund-raising campaign, staff and students of the Department of Chinese and Bilingual Studies of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) has dedicated one of the events in the International Weeks to lend sympathy and support to Japan and its people. The event is titled 'Never Give Up, Japan!' 
Ms Masumi Matsumoto (left), a Japanese Instructor at PolyU, taught participants how to make paper cranes which symbolize hopes and praying for recovery.
During the event, participants sang a song together to extend their best wishes to those affected by the recent tragedy.
