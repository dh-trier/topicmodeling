The Faculty of Engineering of the University of Hong Kong will hold its graduation ceremony on Tuesday, November 30, 2004.Details of the ceremony are as follows:
Date: November 30, 2004 (Tuesday)
Time: 10:00am
Venue: Grand Hall, Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre (New Phase)
The Vice-Chancellor, Professor Lap-Chee Tsui will preside over the ceremony while Mr Paul Chow Man-Yiu, JP, Chief Executive of the Hong Kong Exchanges, will address the assembly.A total of 418 research postgraduate and Master of Science degrees (MSc), and 508 bachelor degrees in Engineering and Computer Science will be awarded.
Reporters and photographers are cordially invited to attend the graduation ceremony.Please bring your name card or pass for identification at the reception.For further enquiries, please contact Mr. Anthony Kam of the Faculty of Engineering Office at 2857 8258.
