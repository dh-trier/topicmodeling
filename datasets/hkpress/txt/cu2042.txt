[date]8 June 2015[/date]
[title]Linsanity 2.0 - Jeremy Lin to Speak on ‘Redefining Success’ at 
The Chinese University of Hong Kong
[/title]
Jeremy Lin, the famous NBA professional basketball player, will come to The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) on 28 June (Sunday) to talk to nearly 2,000 Hong Kong tertiary students on the topic of ‘Redefining Success’.  Free e-tickets will be available for all Hong Kong tertiary students via online registration from 9:00am on 10 June (Wednesday) to 6:00pm on 20 June (Saturday).  Each student is eligible to obtain one ticket, on a first-come-first-served basis.  The online registration website: http://www.cuhk.edu.hk/ccc/chaplaincy/Linsanity2.0/registration_eng.html. 
The event is organized by the Chaplain’s Office of Chung Chi College, CUHK.  Details are as follows:
Linsanity 2.0 – Redefining Success
Date       :       28 June 2015 (Sunday)
Time        :       3:00pm – 5:30pm
Venue      :       Sir Run Run Shaw Hall, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Live)
Y.C. Liang Hall, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Live broadcast)
Speaker   :       Jeremy LIN (NBA Professional Basketball Player)
Respondent:    The Rev. Dr. NG Chun Chi
Language:        English/Putonghua (with simultaneous interpretation)
Enquiries:    chaplaincy@cuhk.edu.hk
On the event day, live webcast will be available at the link below:
http://www.cuhk.edu.hk/ccc/chaplaincy/Linsanity2.0/live_eng.html 
For more details, please refer to the event website: http://www.cuhk.edu.hk/ccc/chaplaincy/Linsanity2.0/index_eng.html.
