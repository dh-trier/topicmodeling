[title]PolyU launches "Music Delight Concert"[/title]
With the start of a new academic year, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) held the "Music Delight Concert ' Plugging in Sem 1" during lunch time today (26 September) to extend a warm welcome to all students. Local pop singers and artists performed in this lively and energetic concert.Professor Philip C.H. Chan, Deputy President and Provost of PolyU, hoped that the "Plugging in Sem 1" Concert could extend a warm welcome to all students.Pop singer &amp; artist Ms Sherman Chung is one of the performers in "Plugging in Sem 1" Concert.Pop singers &amp; artists Ms Hiromi Wada (left) and Mr Bob Cheung (right) sang in the Concert.PolyU Dancing Society (DanSo) performed in the Concert.
