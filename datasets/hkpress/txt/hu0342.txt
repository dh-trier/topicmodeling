[title]HKU to Outline Its Vision for Next 10 year in a Press Briefing Held Tomorrow (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]06 Jul 2005[/date]
 
Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Hong Kong, will outline his vision of the University of Hong Kong for the next five to ten years at a press briefing tomorrow.
Year 2011 is important to HKU not only because the University will celebrate its centenary anniversary in that year, but also because it is when the University will have its totally revamped 4-year curriculum ready to enable the admission to the University of the first batch students under new "3+3+4" academic structure.
Media representatives are cordially invited to the press briefing.A 24-seater will be arranged to pick up the press from Admiralty.Members of the press please wait at the bus stop near Caf de Coral at Admiralty Centre (Exit A of Admiralty MTR station).The shuttle bus will leave at 12:20pm sharp.
Details of the press briefing as follows:
