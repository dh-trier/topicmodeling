[title]PolyU’s MA Graduation Fashion Show - Opening Show of HK Fashion Week for Spring/Summer 2008[/title]
PolyU's MA Graduation Fashion Show - Opening Show of HK Fashion Week for Spring/Summer 2008The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)'s  Institute of Textiles and Clothing (ITC) for the second time organized its Master of Arts Graduation Fashion Show at the Hong Kong Fashion Week for Spring/ Summer 2008 hosted by the Hong Kong Trade Development Council. The Fashion Week is being organized at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre (HKCEC) from 10 to 13 July.
As the opening show of the event, PolyU's Graduation Fashion Show displayed the creative works of its 12 selected graduating students of the 2006-07 Master of Arts in Fashion and Textile Design programme yesterday (10 July). The students each presented a collection of six outfits designed under their chosen themes. The show demonstrated the students' innovation and creativity inspired by their personal visions.
In addition to the Graduation Fashion Show, an exhibition counter, located at the concourse area near the entrance of Hall 1 (Booth No. 1U01), was set up in HKCEC to display the visual works of all 24 graduates of the Master programme.    
ITC is the sole provider of tertiary education in fashion and textiles in Hong Kong. It offers exciting opportunities to those who wish to pursue careers in the dynamic fashion and textiles industry. Internationally recognized as a leading institution in its field, ITC has also earned a fast-growing international reputation as one of the world-class centres in the area of fashion technology.
