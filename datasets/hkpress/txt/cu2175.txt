[date]26 January 2016[/date]
[title]The 6th Global Youth Chinese Literary Award for the New Century
Now Open for Entries[/title]
The Faculty of Arts of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) organizes the Sixth Global Youth Chinese Literary Award this year and held the opening ceremony today (26 January). Officiating at the ceremony were Prof. Joseph J.Y. Sung, Vice-Chancellor; Prof. Leung Yuen-sang, Dean of Arts; Prof. Serena Jin, Emeritus Professor of Translation; Prof. Leo Lee Ou-fan, Sin Wai Kin Professor of Chinese Culture; Prof  Tang Sze-wing, Acting Chairman of Department of Chinese Language and Literature; and Prof. Hoyan Hang-fung, Chairperson of the Organizing Committee of the Award. The competition is now open for online submission until 31 May 2016. For details, please visit www.literary.arts.cuhk.edu.hk.
