[title]Watch World Cup Final at CUHK with Vice-Chancellor Joseph Sung[/title]
Prof. Joseph Sung, Vice-Chancellor of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), will invite all CUHK staff, students and alumni to watch live broadcast of the World Cup Final (14 July) on a giant screen installed at the University Mall.  Mr. Ivan Choy Chi-keung and Prof. Ma Ngok will be the commentators of the match.
