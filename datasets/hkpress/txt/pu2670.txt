[title]PolyU to hold Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now inviting applications for its 2014/15 taught postgraduate programmes. To give prospective applicants first-hand information on its wide range of programmes, PolyU will hold the Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes on 11 January 2014 (Saturday) from 2 p.m. to 6 p.m. on its campus.
Prospective applicants can get in-depth details of the taught postgraduate programmes at more than seventy info seminars or programme consultation sessions organized by the six Faculties of the University: Applied Science and Textiles, Business, Construction and Environment, Engineering, Health and Social Sciences, Humanities, as well as the School of Design and the School of Hotel and Tourism Management.
In the 2014/15 academic year, 77 taught postgraduate programmes will be on offer. For more details of the Info Day, please refer to the Event Guide on web: www.polyu.edu.hk/as/tpginfoday. For details and application procedures of the programmes, please visit the website: www.polyu.edu.hk/study. As all applications received will be considered on a rolling basis, applicants are advised to submit their applications as early as possible.
