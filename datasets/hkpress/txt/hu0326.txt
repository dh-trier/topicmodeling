The University of Hong Kong (HKU) will launch its own consultancy to create e-business value for companies in Hong Kong and Mainland China.ETI Consulting Limited (ETIC) has been established as the consulting arm of the university's R&amp;D organisation, the E-Business Technology Institute (ETI), to facilitate the transfer of the latest e-business technologies and solutions to the community.
Details of the launching ceremony as follows:
Date: May 18, 2004 (Tuesday)
Time: 3:45pm
Venue: Salon 6, Ballroom, JW Marriott Hotel Hong Kong, Pacific Place, 88 Queensway, Hong Kong
Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, Chairman of ETIC and the Vice-Chancellor of HKU and the Hon John Tsang Chun-Wah, JP, Secretary for Commerce, Industry and Technology, will officiate at the ceremony.
Reporters/photographers are cordially invited to attend.For media enquiries, please contact Miss Polo Leung at HKU's External Relations Office at 2859 2600.
