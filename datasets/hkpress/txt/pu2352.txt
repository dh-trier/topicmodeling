[title]Mr Patrick Poon gives ardent support to PolyU [/title]
Mr Patrick Poon Sun-cheong, Chairman of Harvest SCP Group Company Limited, who has been serving as Treasurer of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) since 2008, has kindly agreed to serve as the Chairman of the University's Fund-raising Committee for a term of three years.
Mr Patrick Poon has recently given HK$7.5 million in support of PolyU's research development. Mr Poon said, 'I hope my contribution could set an example and encourage more people to support the future development of PolyU.'
The University's Fund-raising Committee held its first meeting in the 2012-2013 academic year on 13 September. PolyU President Professor Timothy W. Tong expressed his heartfelt gratitude for Mr Patrick Poon's ardent support and generosity, 'PolyU is thankful to Mr Poon and all Fund-raising Committee members, for their active participation in different aspects, valuable advice and unfailing support to PolyU's fund-raising initiatives.'
