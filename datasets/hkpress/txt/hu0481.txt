[title]Convocation of The University of Hong Kong (HKU Convocation)
Extraordinary General Meeting[/title]
[date]31 Aug 2015[/date]
 
The HKU Convocation Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) will be held on Tuesday, September 1, 2015 at 7pm at Hall 3G, Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre (HKCEC). This EGM is convened, pursuant to the Constitution &amp; Rules of Convocation, at three written requests with a total of 6 motions, received by the Chairman of Convocation. Details: www.convocation.hku.hk.
HKU Convocation has engaged BDO Limited to conduct an independent counting of votes.
This EGM will be a closed meeting for HKU Convocation Members only. There will be NO media arrangement inside the EGM meeting venue. A media zone will be set up at the HKCEC Harbour Road Entrance.
The Standing Committee of HKU Convocation will hold a press briefing to announce the voting results of the EGM. Details are as follows:
Date:   September 2, 2015 (Wednesday)
Time:   11am
Venue:  2/F, Function Rooms 4 - 6, Harbourview Hotel, 4 Harbour Road, Wanchai
HKU Convocation is a statutory body established in accordance with The University of Hong Kong Ordinance. It comprises all HKU graduates and teachers.
