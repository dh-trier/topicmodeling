[title]Prof Leo Ou-fan Lee to give talk on "Garden in the City: Towards a Humanistic Architecture"[/title]
[date]03 Dec 2009[/date]
 
The renowned scholar and cultural critic Professor Leo Ou-fan Lee will give a talk on "Garden in the City: Towards a Humanistic Architecture". Professor Leo Lee will explore the possibilities of reinventing a new aesthetic form of architecture in China under the super-modern "impact from the West", and if Hong Kong could rise to the challenge?
The talk will be followed by a roundtable discussion featuring a panel of distinguished architects and curators of the "2009 Hong Kong &amp; Shenzhen Bi-City Biennale of Urbanism\Architecture":
Ou Ning, Chief curator, Shenzhen
Marisa K. S. Yiu, Chief curator, Hong Kong
Pauline J. Yao, Curator, Shenzhen,
Eric H. Schuldenfrei, Curator, Hong Kong 
Date:  December 11, 2009 (Fri)
Time:  5:30pm - 7:30pm
Venue:  Rayson Huang Theatre, The University of Hong Kong
Language: English
Free Admission
