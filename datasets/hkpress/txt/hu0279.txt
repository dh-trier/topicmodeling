[title]HKU to Hold Presentation Ceremony for Students Winning External Competitions (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]21 Apr 2004[/date]
Â 
HKU students who have excelled in competitions over the last year are to be rewarded for their efforts at a ceremony in their honour on Saturday, April 24, 2004.
More than one hundred students from various departments will be recognized of their excellence in their chosen fields of sports, architectural designs, business plans, music composition, speech and debating contests, community services, etc.Certificates of commendation will be presented to the winning students at the ceremony, who have won awards in international competitions, regional competitions and local competitions during the period April 2003 to March 2004.
HKU students stood out in several categories.The HKU team has won the Overall Championship in the 24th Annual Intervarsity Games for the fifth consecutive year.In addition, students in Department of Architecture received many design awards as in previous years.Students also won excellent prizes in business plan competitions, and have received outstanding awards in public speech contests. 
Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Hong Kong and Dr. E John Powell, Dean of Student Affairs, will deliver addresses and present the certificates at the ceremony to be held at the Rayson Huang Theatre, the University of Hong Kong, at 11:00 a.m. on Saturday, April 24, 2004.Reporters/photographers are cordially invited to attend the ceremony.
For media enquiries, please contact Miss Polo Leung at HKU's External Relations Office on 2859 2600.
