[title]HKU holds Long Service Award Presentation Ceremony for senior staff members[/title]
[date]17 Mar 2014[/date]
 


The Long Service Award Presentation Ceremony of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) for academic and senior administrative staff members was held today (March 17, Monday) in Loke Yew Hall. The Vice-Chancellor Professor Lap-Chee Tsui officiated at the ceremony to present awards to over 400 staff members to thank them for their dedication, commitment and loyalty to the University.
In addition to the 40, 35 and 25-year awards, this year’s Long Service Award has been extended to staff members who have served the University for 15 years. The number of awardees amounted to over 400. Among them one member received the 40-year award, five members the 35-year award, 20 members the 25-year award, and 411 members the 15-year award.
