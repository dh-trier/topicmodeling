[title]PolyU invites non-JUPAS applications for its full-time degree and sub-degree programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now accepting non-JUPAS applications for its 2007/08 full-time degree and sub-degree programmes until 16 February. A total of 66 full-time programmes are on offer; while 26 part-time degree and sub-degree programmes will also be open for applications until 5 March.For non-local applicants, applications on the basis of current year JEE results will be accepted until 31 May; while those on the basis of other non-local qualifications will be accepted until 31 March.Among the full-time programmes, 15 are double degree programmes which combine the strengths of different academic disciplines. Students can obtain a second degree in a different academic discipline by spending an extra year on a self-financed basis.In response to the rising demand for professional manpower in the finance industry, a new full-time programme entitled Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Investment Science will be launched. It aims to equip students with strong mathematical and statistical skills and thorough understanding of their applications in investment and finance, preparing students for careers in the finance industry.All PolyU programmes are operating on a credit-based structure which allows greater flexibility in both the selection of subjects and pace of study. In addition, starting from 2005/2006, all full-time programmes have included a mandatory work-integrated education component, through which students will gain valuable workplace learning experience during their studies.Other than degree and sub-degree programmes, a series of taught postgraduate programmes are also on offer with varied application deadlines. For more details on PolyU programmes and application procedures, please visit the official website at URL: https://www.polyu.edu.hk/study. Hardcopies of the Admission Handbooks are also obtainable at the Academic Secretariat Service Centre during the application period.Applicants are required to submit their applications online at the above listed webpage. As all applications received will be considered on a rolling basis, they are advised to submit their applications as early as possible.Enquiry Hotline: 2333 0600
