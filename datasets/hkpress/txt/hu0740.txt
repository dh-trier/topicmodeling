[title]HKU's Faculty of Education is to Hold its Graduation Ceremony 2004 (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]20 Nov 2004[/date]
Â 
The Faculty of Education of the University of Hong Kong will hold its graduation ceremony on Monday, November 22, 2004 at 10:30am at the Concert Hall, Hong Kong Culture Centre, Tsimshatsui, Kowloon.
Dr Meng Hongwei of the China National Institute for Educational Research will be the guest of honour while Professor Richard Y C Wong, Deputy Vice-Chancellor of HKU, will preside over the Ceremony.One award, seven prizes and one scholarship will be presented to outstanding students.The Faculty of Education has a total of 262 higher degree graduands, 215 undergraduands, and 763 graduands of Postgraduate Certificate in Advanced Education Studies and Postgraduate Certificate in Education this year.
For further enquiries, please contact Ms Annie Lai (Tel: 2241 5414) at Faculty of Education.
