[title]PolyU to hold Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now inviting applications for its 2015/16 taught postgraduate programmes. To give prospective applicants first-hand information on its wide range of programmes, PolyU will hold the Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes on 10 January 2015 (Saturday) from 2 p.m. to 6 p.m. on its campus.
Prospective applicants can get detailed information about the taught postgraduate programmes at more than eighty info seminars or programme consultation sessions organized by the six Faculties of the University: Applied Science and Textiles, Business, Construction and Environment, Engineering, Health and Social Sciences, Humanities, as well as the School of Design and the School of Hotel and Tourism Management.
In the 2015/16 academic year, 83 taught postgraduate programmes will be on offer, including the following six new programmes:
Executive Master in Meaningful Innovation
MA in Bilingual Corporate Communication
MA in Japanese Media and Communication
MSc in Global Hospitality Business
MSc / PgD in International Wine Management
MSc in Mental Health Nursing
