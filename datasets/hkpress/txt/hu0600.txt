[title]University of Glasgow delegation meets with HKU senior management[/title]
[date]11 May 2016[/date]
 


One of the largest ever academic delegations to represent the University of Glasgow visited Hong Kong to establish new links and reinforce existing links with the University of Hong Kong. The 15 academics from the University of Glasgow – covering all four of its academic schools – – Medical, Veterinary and Life Sciences; Social Sciences; Arts; and Science and Engineering –spent three days in Hong Kong from 9th to 11th May 2016. The delegation met with HKU President Professor Peter Mathieson, Vice President (Global) Professor W. John Kao and Dean of Medicine Professor Gabriel M Leung on 9th May 2016 on HKU main campus.
