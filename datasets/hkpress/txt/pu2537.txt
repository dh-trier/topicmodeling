[title]PolyU and WU Co-organise Knowledge Management Workshop[/title]
The Faculty of Business at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) and the Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU) jointly organised a workshop on 22 May 2013 Wednesday. The one-day event, themed Knowledge Management across Borders: A Chinese/Austrian Perspective, featured recent research findings about global knowledge management in organisations and practices of Austrian/European subsidiaries in Hong Kong and mainland China. 
(From left) Professor Edwin Cheng, Dean of the Faculty of Business at PolyU; Mr Everist Limaj, from the Institute for Information Management and Control at WU; Dr Loo Geok Pee, Assistant Professor at the Tokyo Institute of Technology; and Professor Edward W. N. Bernroider, University Professor and Head of Institute for Information Management and Control at WU.
 
Mr Christian H. Schierer, Regional Austrian Trade Commissioner for Hong Kong, Macao and South China, talked about strategies and challenges of knowledge management in China.
