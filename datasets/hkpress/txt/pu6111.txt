[title]PolyU Admission Strategies for JUPAS Applicants[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University will hold an event "PolyU Admission Strategies for JUPAS Applicants" on campus from 12:30 to 5:00pm on 16 May 2015 (Saturday).
JUPAS applicants can make good use of this occasion to obtain the latest updates on PolyU programmes before they decide to reprioritize their JUPAS programme choices. Various activities will be arranged during the day, including consultation sessions, info seminars, video shows, guided visits and exhibitions.
More information about the Admission Strategies for JUPAS Applicants can be found at www.polyu.edu.hk/as/strategies2015.
