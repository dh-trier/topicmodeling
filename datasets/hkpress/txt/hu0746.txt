[title]HKU Council makes public the Audit Committee’s report on donations[/title]
[date]30 Jun 2015[/date]
 
The Council of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) today (June 30) resolved to make public the documents relating to the Audit Committee’s report.
The documents can be downloaded at:
http://www.gs.hku.hk/report/30062015-documents.pdf.
