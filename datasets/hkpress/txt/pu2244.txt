[title]PolyU receives $8M donation to set up "Ho and Ho Foundation Scholarship"[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) has recently received a generous donation of HK$8 million from Ho and Ho Foundation Ltd to set up the "Ho and Ho Foundation Scholarship". The scholarship will be used to support mainland students with outstanding performance, in particular those from the 'Red Triangle' areas (Shaoguan of Guangdong province, Ganzhou of Jiangxi province and Chenzhou of Hunan province), for pursuing their studies at PolyU.
The agreement on the setting up of "Ho and Ho Foundation Scholarship" was signed by Mr Ho Ming SZE (right), Life Honorary Chairman of Ho and Ho Foundation Ltd., and Professor Walter W. Yuen, PolyU Vice President (Academic Development).
The signing ceremony was attended by (from left): Mr Tang Lai Po; Dr Laura Lo, Director of Chinese Mainland Affairs, PolyU; Miss Vivian Ho; Professor Otto Lin, Senior Advisor to President, PolyU; Professor Walter W.
