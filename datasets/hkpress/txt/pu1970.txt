[title]PolyU celebrates Mid-Autumn Festival with non-local students[/title]
To give non-local students a taste of the festive atmosphere, Professor Timothy W. Tong, President of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), and other guests celebrated the Mid-Autumn Festival with moon cakes sponsored by Taipan Bread &amp; Cakes Co., LTD at the hostel in Shantung Street, Mongkok last night (21 September).
All the participants have an enjoyable evening sharing moon cakes and other festive food.
One of the highlights of the gathering was students playing Guzheng-Erhu duet, earning loud applause from the audience.
President Professor Timothy W. Tong presenting a box of Taipan Snowy Moon Cakes to representative of each flat.
Special thanks to Taipan Bread &amp; Cakes Co., LTD for sponsoring boxes of Taipan Snowy Moon Cakes in this joyous occasion.
Also joining the occasion were: (second row, fourth from left) Ms Tracy Ng, Director of Communications and Public Affairs; Mrs Dorinda Fung, Director of Student Affairs; Professor Arthur Mak, Dean of Students; Landlord Mr Daniel K.C. Chan; President Professor Timothy W. Tong; PolyU alumnus Mr Tsui Tack-kong; and Vice President (Institutional Advancement and Partnership) Professor Angelina Yuen.
