[title]PolyU to hold Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now inviting applications for its 2012/13 taught postgraduate programmes. To give prospective applicants first-hand information on its wide range of programmes, around 50 info seminars/programme consultation sessions will be held on the Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes on 7 January 2012 (Saturday) from 2 p.m. to 6 p.m. on its campus.
The Info Day will give prospective applicants in-depth details of the programmes at the on-campus information seminars/programme consultation sessions organized by five Faculties namely Applied Science and Textiles, Construction and Environment, Engineering, Health and Social Sciences, Humanities, as well as the School of Design and School of Hotel and Tourism Management.
In the 2012/13 academic year, 72 taught postgraduate programmes will be on offer, including the following three new programmes (subject to the Senate's approval):

Executive Master in Global Hospitality Leadership

For more details of the Info Day, please refer to the Event Guide on web: www.polyu.edu.hk/as/tpginfoday. For details and application procedures of the programmes, please visit the website: www.polyu.edu.hk/study. As all applications received will be considered on a rolling basis, applicants are advised to submit their applications as early as possible.
The Faculty of Business will also organize separate Information Seminars. Details will be available later on the website of the Graduate School of Business: www.polyu.edu.hk/gsb
For more information, please call Enquiry Hotline: (852) 2333 0600.
