[title]Double happiness for PolyU Students' Union[/title]
This year not only marks the 75th anniversary of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), but also the 40th anniversary of its Students' Union. In celebration of the joyous occasion, the Students' Union held a "poon choi" dinner on 11 February which was joined by around 900 PolyU students, staff members and alumni.
The celebration dinner was graced with the presence of Mrs Rita Fan, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. Addressing the occasion, Mrs Fan encouraged university students to sharpen their competitiveness and rise to the challenges ahead.
The Honourable Marjorie Yang Mun-tak, PolyU Council Chairman, and Professor Timothy W. Tong, PolyU President officially kicked off the celebration dinner by beating the gong. (From left) Professor Keith C. C. Chan, Acting Dean of Students; Professor Angelina Yuen, Vice President (Institutional Advancement and Partnership); Mrs Rita Fan;; Professor Judy Tsui, Vice President (International and Executive Education); Professor Walter W. Yuen, Vice President (Academic Development); and Mr Frank Hung, President of PolyU Students' Union.
Special programmes were put up for the occasion including a live band performance by the Music Society and orchestral music presented by the Chinese Orchestra of the Students' Union. Their shows drew loud applause from the audience.
