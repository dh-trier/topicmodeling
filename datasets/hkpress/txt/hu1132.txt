[title]Presentation Ceremony of Prizes and Awards for Faculty of Dentistry and Prince Philip Dental Hospital[/title]
[date]20 Nov 2003[/date]
Â 
The Faculty of Dentistry of the University of Hong Kong in conjunction with the Board of Governors of The Prince Philip Dental Hospital, will hold a Presentation Ceremony of Prizes and Awards on Monday, November 24, 2003 at 5:30 p.m. in Meeting Room 601, the Sixth Floor, the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre, 1 Expo Drive, Wanchai, Hong Kong.
At the Ceremony jointly hosted by the Faculty and the Hospital, prizes and awards will be presented to about 100 dental students and ancillary trainees.
Among the presenters of the prizes and awards will be The Hon. Mak Kwok-fung, Legislative Councillor, Professor J.A. Spinks, Pro-Vice-Chancellor (Academic) of the University of Hong Kong, Mr. Andrew K.Y. Leung, Chairman of the Hospital Board of Governors, Professor F.C. Smales, Faculty Dean and Hospital Director, and Dr. E.K.K. Chan, President of the Hong Kong Dental Association.Many representatives from the University of Hong Kong and Hong Kong's dental community will be present.
You are cordially invited to send journalists to cover the above event.
