[title]PolyU President celebrates Lunar New Year with Development Foundation members [/title]
Professor Timothy W. Tong, President of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, hosted a Lunar New Year Luncheon for members of the PolyU Development Foundation today (17 February) in celebration of the Year of the Rabbit.
 
Professor Tong expressed his heartfelt thanks to all members of the foundation for their ardent support to PolyU that is vital in contributing to its development in pursuit of academic excellence. 
 
Before the luncheon, a meeting was held on the university campus where members of the foundation were briefed on the latest development and achievements of the University.
