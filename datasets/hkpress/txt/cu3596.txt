[title]Third Honorary Fellowship Conferment Ceremony[/title]

[date]14 May 2004[/date]


 The Chinese University of Hong Kong 
 Third Honorary Fellowship Conferment Ceremony 
 
The Third Honorary Fellowship Conferment Ceremony of The Chinese University 
 of Hong Kong will take place at 5:00 pm on Monday, 17 May 2004 at Lee 
 Hysan Concert Hall, Esther Lee Building on the campus. Six distinguished 
 persons who are closely associated with CUHK will be conferred Honorary 
 Fellowship in recognition of their significant contributions to the 
 University and the community. They are: 
Mrs 
 CHAN WONG Shui, Pamela 
Dr CHEN Tseng Tao, Thomas
Mr LEE Ming Kwai, Dick
Mr PANG Yuk Wing, Joseph
Professor John Nelson TARN 
 
Dr YUNG Kung Hing
