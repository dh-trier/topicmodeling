[title]President Tong will attend the Military Parade in Beijing tomorrow[/title]
In response to a number of media enquiries received this morning on whether President, Professor Timothy W. Tong will attend the military parade in Beijing tomorrow (3 September), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University would like to clarify that Professor Tong has received the invitation earlier and will attend the military parade in Beijing tomorrow in the capacity of Committee Member of The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.
