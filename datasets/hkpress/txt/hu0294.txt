[title]Award Presentation Ceremony for Excellence in Teaching, Research and Knowledge Exchange 2015[/title]
[date]09 May 2016[/date]
Â 


The University of Hong Kong (HKU) held an Award Presentation Ceremony today (May 9) to honour prominent teachers and researchers with University teaching and research awards for excellence in their fields as well as to recognise their outstanding knowledge exchange activities.
At the ceremony, 12 categories of awards were conferred upon a number of our colleagues including the Faculty Teaching Awards, Research Output Prize, Faculty Knowledge Exchange Award, Outstanding Research Student Supervisor Award, Outstanding Young Researcher Award, Outstanding Researcher Award, Teaching Innovation Award, Outstanding Teaching Award, Knowledge Exchange Excellence Award, Distinguished Research Achievement Award, University Distinguished Teaching Award and Member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
For further information, please visit: http://www.hku.hk/award/.
