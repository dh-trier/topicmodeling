[title]Live webcast of the University of Hong Kong Centenary Ceremony[/title]
[date]16 Aug 2011[/date]
 
Members of the public will be able to watch the entire University of Hong Kong Centenary Ceremony live online from 9:30am to 10:30am on Thursday, August 18, 2011.
The ceremony will be held in Loke Yew Hall and officiated by the Chancellor, Dr the Hon Donald Tsang. Vice-Premier Li Keqiang of the State Council, and Lord David Wilson, Chancellor of the University of Aberdeen and HKU alumnus, will be the Guests of Honour and will address the ceremony.
A total of 600 staff, students, alumni and guests will attend the ceremony. Among the guests are Vice-chancellors/Presidents from 17 local, mainland and overseas universities. 
For media enquiries, please contact Ms Trinni Choy, Assistant Director (Media) (Tel: 2859 2606/Email: pychoy@hku.hk).
