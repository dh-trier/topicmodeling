[title]PolyU holds Consultation Day for JUPAS Applicants 2014[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) will be holding a Consultation Day for Joint University Programmes Admissions System (JUPAS) applicants on 17 May 2014 (Saturday) from 1:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m.  JUPAS Applicants can make good use of this occasion to obtain the latest updates on PolyU programmes before they decide their final JUPAS programme choices. 
The event will provide JPUAS applicants with an opportunity to visit PolyU campus and facilities, to learn about the admissions procedures, and to familiarize themselves with PolyU programmes through consultation sessions, information seminars, video shows, guided visits and exhibitions offered by various academic departments.
The following special information seminars will also be arranged:
(i) "Strategy for Programme Selection" will cover the calculation of admissions scores, scholarships for JUPAS admittees and the tactics for programme choice modification.  PolyU Student Ambassadors will also share their experiences on programme selection and their study life at PolyU.
(ii) "Tips for Admission Interview" will be conducted by experienced PolyU Professor to help students make good preparation and polish their interview skills for their admission interviews.
More information about the Consultation Day can be found at  www.polyu.edu.hk/as/conday2014.
Notes to Editor
Members of the media are cordially invited to attend and cover the event. If typhoon signal no. 8 (or above) or rainstorm black warning is in force at 10:00 a.m. on the day, the event will be rescheduled 18 May 2014 (Sunday).
