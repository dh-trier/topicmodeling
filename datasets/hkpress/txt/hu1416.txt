[title]Vice-Chancellor's Cup Soccer Match 2013 celebrates joint winners[/title]
[date]24 Mar 2013[/date]
 

















The Univeristy of Hong Kong (HKU) and the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) were winners in the annual Vice-Chancellor's Cup Soccer Match today (March 24) after both sides played to a 1:1 tie in the game.
HKU Vice-Chancellor Professor Lap-Chee Tsui and CU Vice-Chancellor Professor Joseph J.Y. Sung officiated at the kickoff ceremony  held in HKU's Stanley Ho Sports Centre at Sandy Bay.
The soccer match has been played for the tenth year since its launch in 2003 to commemorate the collaboration of the two universities during the SARS ordeal. Joint winners have been recorded in three counts, CUHK captured a total of six titles, HKU won in one game.
