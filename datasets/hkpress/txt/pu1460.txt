[title]PolyU's degree and sub-degree programmes invite applications[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now accepting applications for its 2004/05 full-time and part-time bachelor's degree and sub-degree programmes through non-JUPAS admission. A total of 56 full-time and 40 part-time programmes are open for applications until 16 June.
Two full-time programmes to be offered for the first time are: the three-year UGC-funded Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Medical Laboratory Science and the two-year self-financed Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in Product Engineering with Marketing.
In addition, seven new part-time programmes will be offered in the coming academic year. They are: Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in Electrical Engineering, Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in Electronic and Information Engineering, Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in Product Engineering with Marketing, Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Environmental and Occupational Safety and Health, Bachelor of Arts (Honours) Scheme in Fashion and Textile Studies comprising three specialisms of study namely Fashion and Textile Design, Fashion and Textile Marketing and Merchandising, and Fashion Retailing.
