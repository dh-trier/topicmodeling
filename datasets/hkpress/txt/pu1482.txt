[title]PolyU invites final applications to its 2003/04 programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is inviting applications to a handful of unfilled places in some of its programmes for the academic year 2003/04. Application will close on 28 July.
Programmes now open for application include part-time bachelor's degree, sub-degree programmmes, as well as taught postgraduate programmes. Most of these programmes are self-financed programmes which will commence class in the coming September.
The public can obtain application details by visiting the University's Website at http://www.polyu.edu.hk/admission. Application information are also available through the following:
via enquiry hotline at 2333 0600 (for programme information only)
by e-mail to asadmpg@polyu.edu.hk for taught postgraduate programmes, asadmpt@polyu.edu.hk for part-time bachelor's degree and sub-degree programmes
at the Academic Secretariat Service Centre, Room M101, Li Ka Shing Tower on PolyU campus
