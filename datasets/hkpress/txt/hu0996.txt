[title]HKU to Deliver Seminars during “Education and Careers Expo 2003”[/title]
[date]19 Feb 2003[/date]
 
HKU, again in partnership with the other seven local Universities, will mount a “Hong Kong Higher Education Pavilion” at the “Education and Careers Expo 2003” in the Hall 7 of the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre from February 20 – 23, 2003.
Besides having a booth exhibition, HKU will hold three seminar sessions in Room 608 at the HKCEC. Details of the seminars are as follows:
(1) Date:February 21, 2003 (Friday)
Time:11:00 - 11:50 am
Topic:Beyond the Evolution of Technology, Managing Knowledge for Business: MSc in E-Commerce and Internet Computing at HKU
Speaker:Dr Paul Cheung, Programme Director, MSc in E-Commerce and Internet Computing, HKU
(2) Date:February 21, 2003 (Friday)
Time:3:40 - 4:30 pm 
Topic:How do University Graduates Breakthrough the Career Challenge?
Speaker:Ms Louisa Li, Director, Careers Education &amp; Placement Centre, HKU
(3) Date:February 23, 2003 (Sunday) 
Time:11:00 - 11:50 am 
Topic:Postgraduate Education for a Knowledge-based Economy at the University of Hong Kong
Speaker:Professor Anthony Yeh, Dean, Graduate School, HKU
Members of the press are cordially invited to attend. The seminars are conducted in English but questions in Chinese are also welcome. 
For media enquiries, please contact Miss Leung at 2859 2600.
