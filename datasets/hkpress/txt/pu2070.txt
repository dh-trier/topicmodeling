[title]Top official from Ministry of Education of the Chinese mainland visits PolyU[/title]
Madam Zhang Xiuqin (first row, sixth from left), Director General of Department of International Cooperation and Exchange, Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China visits Hong Kong and holds a roundtable meeting at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). The meeting, joined by senior management of PolyU and representatives from local tertiary institutions, helps enhance the exchanges and collaborations between institutions in Hong Kong and the Chinese mainland.
President Timothy W. Tong (second from right) welcomes the visit of Madam Zhang. The delegation also includes Mr Song Bo (left), Deputy Director, Office for Hong Kong, Macao &amp; Taiwan Affairs, Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China. Ms Michelle Li Mei-sheung (middle), Deputy Secretary for Education, Education Bureau of HKSAR and Prof. Angelina Yuen (second from left), Vice President (Institutional Advancement and Partnership) also join the meeting to share their views on future collaborations between institutions.
At the roundtable meeting, Madam Zhang talks about the education development plan of the Chinese mainland in coming ten years. After the meeting, Madam Zhang visits the new premises of PolyU School of Hotel and Tourism Management (SHTM). From left: Prof. Song Haiyan, Chair Professor of SHTM; Mr Song Bo; Ms Michelle Li Mei-sheung; Madam Zhang; Prof. Angelina Yuen; Prof. Kay Chon, Dean of SHTM; Dr Laura Lo, Director of PolyU Chinese Mainland Affairs Office.
