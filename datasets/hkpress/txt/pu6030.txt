[title]PolyU announces re-appointment of Professor Philip C.H. Chan as Deputy President and Provost[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is pleased to announce today (24 September 2014) the re-appointment of Professor Philip C.H. Chan as Deputy President and Provost for three years. Professor Chan's second term will commence on 1 March 2015. The re-appointment was approved at the Council meeting today. Input had been sought from various stakeholders, including members of the management team, representatives of the Students' Union and the PolyU Staff Association, before the re-appointment was recommended to Council.
Professor Chan joined PolyU on 1 March 2010 and is responsible for the overall planning, development and operation of the University's academic enterprise which includes learning and teaching, research activities, professional and continuing education, as well as student affairs.
"Professor Chan has made major contributions to education and research in PolyU over the past four years. I am sure that with the expertise and commitment of Professor Chan, PolyU will be able to achieve even greater success," said Ms Marjorie Yang, Council Chairman.
"I am thankful to Professor Chan for his effort in steering the smooth implementation of the new undergraduate curriculum and the development of an inspiring and nurturing environment for our faculty members and students. I look forward to working closely with him in the coming years to realize our vision and mission," said Professor Timothy W. Tong, President of PolyU.
