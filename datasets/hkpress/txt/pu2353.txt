[title]PolyU receives donation from Lee Kum Kee Family Foundation[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) has recently received a generous donation of HK$1 million from Lee Kum Kee Family Foundation in support of the University's service-learning projects for promoting cross-generational harmony.
 A cheque presentation ceremony was held on 17 September 2012. PolyU President Professor Timothy W. Tong (left) expressed his heartfelt gratitude to Mr Lee Man Tat, Group Chairman of Lee Kum Kee, and received a cheque from him on behalf of the University.
 
The presentation ceremony was also attended by Mrs Lee Man Tat (fourth from right); Mrs Elizabeth Mok (third from left), Chairman of Lee Kum Kee Family Foundation; Mr Harry Yeung (second from left), Senior Vice President of LKK Health Products Group Ltd.; Professor Angelina Yuen (third from right), PolyU Vice President (Institutional Advancement and Partnership); Professor Keith Chan (first from left), Dean of Students; and Ms Kitty Yam (second from right), Director of Alumni Affairs and Development.
