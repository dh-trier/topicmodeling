[date]6 February 2014[/date]
[title]CUHK Held Chinese New Year Media Reception Today[/title]
Prof. Joseph J.Y. Sung, Vice-Chancellor and President, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), hosted a Chinese New Year Media Reception at the Vice-Chancellor’s Lodge today (6 February) together with Provost, Pro-Vice-Chancellors and Faculty Deans.  Professor Sung joined the international students and students of New Asia Chinese Music Society to present a Chinese music performance to media friends, wishing them an auspicious year of the Horse.  The host party also had fun in the birthday cake decoration game to celebrate the traditional ‘Renri’ (Everybody’s Birthday).
