[title]PolyU's quality taught postgraduate programmes on offer[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now accepting applications for 48 quality taught postgraduate programmes until 22 July 2004.
The taught postgraduate programmes of PolyU are practical and professionally-oriented to enhance the competitiveness of students to suit the needs of industry, business and the professions. All programmes are run by its six flagship faculties and the School of Hotel and Tourism Management ranging from engineering to business administration, fashion and design, social work and various healthcare studies.
PolyU now offers seven brand new self-financed postgraduate programmes which are in line with the market needs. They include: six part-time programmes ― comprising Doctor of Health Science, Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Applied Mathematics for Science and Technology (Decision Science), Master of Corporate Governance, Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Construction IT, Master of Science in Multimedia and Entertainment Technology (extended mode), and Postgraduate Diploma in Chinese Language and Literature ― and the full-time Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Applied Mathematics for Science and Technology (Actuarial &amp; Investment Science).
The public can obtain application details simply by visiting the University's Website at http://www.polyu.edu.hk/admission or one of the following ways:
