[title]Construction and Environment alumni pitch in for 75th anniversary celebration[/title]
Some 280 guests, alumni, friends and industry partners of the Faculty of Construction and Environment (FCE) of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) gathered at Hotel ICON last Saturday (18 February) to join the FCE Alumni Associations' Ball, a special event celebrating the University's 75th anniversary.
The ball was jointly organized by the five FCE Alumni Associations'Hong Kong Polytechnic University Building Association, Building and Real Estate Departmental Alumni Association, Alumni Association of Building Services Engineering, Civil and Structural Engineering Alumni Association, and Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics Alumni Association.
The joyous event was kicked off with a cake-cutting ceremony by the Honourable Marjorie Yang Mun-tak (third from right), Chairman of PolyU Council; Ir Professor Johnny Fan (second from left), Chairman of the ball and President of The Federation of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University Alumni Associations; Professor Timothy W. Tong (first from left), PolyU President; and Professor Teng Jin-Guang (right), FCE Dean.
The ball was also joined by: (fourth from left) Mr Nicholas W. Yang, Executive Vice President; Dr Dan Waters, former Head of the Department of Building, Surveying &amp; Structural Engineering of the Hong Kong Technical College; Professor Philip Chan, Deputy President and Provost; Professor Timothy W. Tong; Ir Professor Johnny Fan; the Honourable Marjorie Yang Mun-tak; Professor Teng Jin-Guang; Professor Michael Anson and Professor Ko Jan-ming, former Deans of the Faculty; Ir Professor Fred Ng, former Head of the Department of Building, Surveying &amp; Structural Engineering of the Hong Kong Polytechnic; Professor Angelina Yuen, Vice President (Institutional Advancement and Partnership); Professor Judy Tsui, Vice President (International and Executive Education); Ms Kitty Yam, Director of Alumni Affairs and Development; Professor Alex Wai, Vice President (Research Development), and organising committee members.
Professor Tong expressed his heartfelt thanks for alumnus Ir Tsui Tak-kong for being the major sponsor of this memorable occasion.
