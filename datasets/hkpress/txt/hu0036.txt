[title]TWENTY STAFF AT HKU RECEIVED LONG SERVICE AWARDS[/title]
[date]28 Apr 2006[/date]
 
A total of 20 senior staff of The University of Hong Kong who have served the University for over 25 years received long service awards in a ceremony today (April 28, 2006).
The ceremony is held annually by the University for our academic and senior administrative staff and it is going into its thirteenth year now. Those to be awarded include teachers and professionals from various academic and support service departments. (please refer to the enclosed for list of recipients.)
For photos of today's event, please download at http://www.hku.hk/hkumedia/ and check icon "Events" under "Photo Gallery" after 6:00pm.
For enquiries please contact Ms. Cherry Cheung, Senior Press Officer HKU at 2859 2606.
