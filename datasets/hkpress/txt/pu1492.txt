[title]PolyU invites applications to its postgraduate programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now accepting application for admission to its taught postgraduate programmes until 17 March 2003.  The taught postgraduate programmes of PolyU are practical and professionally-oriented to enhance the competitiveness of students to suit the needs of industry, business and the professions.The University provides a wide range of academic programmes, ranging from engineering to business administration, fashion and design, social work and various healthcare studies.  All programmes are run by its 26 academic departments, operated under six flagship faculties.  In its last admission exercise, PolyU received the highest number of applications for the postgraduate programmes of Information Technology, China Business Studies, MBA (General Management) and Professional Accounting.  The public can obtain the application details simply by visiting the University's Website at http://www.polyu.edu.hk/admission. Applicants can also obtain application information through the following:- via enquiry hotline at 2333 0600 (for programme information only; application form and Guide for Applicants can be downloaded from the above website or requested by writing to the following address)- by e-mail to asadmpg@polyu.edu.hk- in writing to Admissions and Promotions Section, Academic Secretariat, PolyU, Hung Hom- in person at the Academic Secretariat Podium Office (Room AG205) on PolyU campus. Opening hours of the counter from 7 February to 17 March 2003 are:   Mondays to Fridays: 9.00 a.m. - 5.00 p.m.   Saturdays: 9.00 a.m. - 12.00 noon   Sundays and Public Holidays: Closed PolyU provides a wide range of postgraduate programmes, including MPhil and PhD research degree programmes as well as taught postgraduate programmes.
