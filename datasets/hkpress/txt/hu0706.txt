[title]HKU Civil Engineering Students To Receive Scholarships[/title]
[date]18 Feb 2003[/date]
Â 
Twenty-three students from the Department of Civil Engineering of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) are to receive scholarships at the ceremony held on February 20, 2003 at 5:30p.m. in the Eliot Room, 14/F, K.K. Leung Building, The University of Hong Kong.
Souvenirs will be presented to all sponsors by the Vice-Chancellor Professor Lap-Chee Tsui at the ceremony for their generosity in offering contributions to the cause of academic excellence.Professor Y.K. Cheung, Honorary Professor of the Department of Civil Engineering, will address the assembly.
A full list of the awardees of the scholarships is attached.Reporters/photographers are cordially invited to attend.
