[title]Three Distinguished CASS Scholars to Share Latest Developments in Sustainability, Social Governance and Linguistics Research at CUHK
All are Welcome[/title]
The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) will host the fifth Chinese Academy of Social Sciences (CASS) Scholars Visit Programme from 8 to 11 March 2016. Three distinguished CASS scholars will visit CUHK to forge academic exchange in humanities and social science. The programme presents a valuable opportunity to strengthen the tie between CUHK and CASS in terms of high-level research exchanges, paving the way for further collaboration.
The CASS delegation comprises Prof. Pan Jiahua from the Institute of Urban and Environmental Studies, Prof. Wang Junxiu from the Institute of Sociology and Prof. Hu Jianhua from the Institute of Linguistics. They will visit relevant departments and research institutes of CUHK for academic exchange and deliver public lectures on 9 and 10 March to share insights into the cutting edge of their chosen fields in sustainability, social governance and linguistics. 
The lectures will be conducted in Putonghua. Members of the public are welcome to attend and register at http://www.cuhk.edu.hk/oalc/CASS_2016/.
