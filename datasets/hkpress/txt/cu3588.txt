 Return of The Sun Hung Kai Properties Nobel Laureates Distinguished Lectures 
 World Renowned Scientist Uncovers Wonders of Microscopic World
  
 
 Sun Hung Kai Properties and The Chinese University of Hong Kong have invited Dr. Richard J. Roberts, 1993 Nobel Laureate in Physiology or Medicine, to deliver a public lecture on "Friends and Foes-the Unseen Bugs Who Share Our Planet. In the lecture, Dr. Roberts will uncover the wonders of the microscopic world with academics, students, and the public.
The previous two lecture series were extremely well received. Academics, students and members of the public were enlightened by the insights of world class scholars. This time, the organizers have also reserved a limited number of free tickets for the public.
