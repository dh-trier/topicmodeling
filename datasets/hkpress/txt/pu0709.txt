[title]PolyU celebrates inauguration of new overseas association in Northern California[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) celebrated the inauguration of its sixth overseas association in the US recently. Named The Hong Kong Polytechnic University Association (Northern California), the new body is formed to unite PolyU graduates and past employees in Northern California, US and foster their links with the University.
The inauguration ceremony of this Association was held in San Francisco on 22 October with the presence of Prof. Poon Chung-kwong, President, and Ir Prof. Ko Jan-ming, Vice President (Research Development) of PolyU. Five other overseas PolyU associations were previously established in Canada, Australia, New York of the US and Singapore since 1996. 
The first Board of Directors of the Association includes the following members:  President: Mr Paul Lee Vice President: Mr Victor Tsui Secretary: Mrs Cecilia Yeung Chief Financial Officer: Mr Eddie Kwong Directors: Mr Wing Ko, Ms Kathy Liu Financial Consultant: Mr Colin Chui
The Association aims at fostering ties among the graduates and former staff members of PolyU or its forerunners (including The Hong Kong Polytechnic, the Hong Kong Technical College and the Hong Kong Government Trade School) residing in Northern California.  Apart from fully supporting the goals and activities of PolyU, the Association will also actively participate in the intellectual and cultural activities in the US.   Meanwhile, it also serves to encourage new migrants from Hong Kong to integrate with the local community. 
All graduates and former staff residing in Northern California are welcomed to join the association. For more details of the Association or to obtain a copy of the application form, please contact the association:
