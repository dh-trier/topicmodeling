[title]PolyU receives a generous donation of $5 million[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) has recently received a generous donation of $5 million from Mr Stephen Lau, Chairman of Kimberley Hotel, in support of the campus development of the University.
PolyU President Professor Timothy W. Tong expressed his heartfelt gratitude to Mr Lau's generosity. He said that Mr Lau's benefaction will help PolyU to develop a more sustainable and environmentally friendly campus.
Mr Yau Wah-yau (right), Chairman of PolyU's Fund-raising Committee, presented a souvenir to Mr Lau as a special token of appreciation.
