[title]PolyU DBA/DMgt Nurture Scholar-leaders[/title]
A reception for the graduates of PolyU DBA (Doctor of Business Administration) (Hong Kong) and DMgt (Doctor of Management) (Mainland China) programmes was organised by the Faculty of Business of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) today (25 October). Eighteen “scholar-leaders” have gone through an intellectual transformation and will apply their professional management knowledge, leadership and research skills to tackle real-world problems.
PolyU President Professor Timothy W. Tong (front middle), Vice President (International and Executive Education) Professor Judy Tsui (front fifth from right), Faculty of Business Dean Professor Edwin Cheng (front fifth from left), Associate Dean Dr Man-kwong Leung (front third from right), Head of School of Accounting &amp; Finance Professor Agnes Cheng (front second from right), Head of Logistics &amp; Maritime Studies Professor Chung-Lun Li (front fourth from left), Renmin University of China Professor Liu Jun(front fourth from right), DBA/DMgt Programme Director Dr JJ Hsieh (front third from left), teachers and graduates.
PolyU President Professor Timothy W. Tong (middle), Vice President (International and Executive Education) Professor Judy Tsui (third from right), Faculty of Business Dean Professor Edwin Cheng (third from left), Renmin University of China Professor Liu Jun (second from right), DBA/DMgt Programme Director Dr JJ Hsieh (second from left), DBA Best Thesis Awardee Wu Chun Wah (first from right) and DMgt Thesis Awardee Chen Shengjie (first from left).
