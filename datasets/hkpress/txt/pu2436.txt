[title]PolyU to hold Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now inviting applications for its 2013/14 taught postgraduate programmes. To give prospective applicants first-hand information on its wide range of programmes, more than 70 info seminars/programme consultation sessions will be held on the Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes on 19 January 2013 (Saturday) from 2 p.m. to 6 p.m. on its campus.
Prospective applicants can get in-depth details of the programmes at the information seminars/programme consultation sessions organized by the six Faculties: Applied Science and Textiles, Business, Construction and Environment, Engineering, Health and Social Sciences, Humanities, as well as the School of Design and School of Hotel and Tourism Management.
In the 2013/14 academic year, 79 taught postgraduate programmes will be on offer, including the following four new programmes:
Master of Design (International Design and Business Management)#
Master of Science/Postgraduate Diploma in High Performance Buildings
Master of Science in Operations Research and Risk Analysis
Master of Speech Therapy
# This is a new specialism added to the existing Master's degree programme. The offer of this programme is subject to Senate's approval.
For more details of the Info Day, please refer to the Event Guide on web: www.polyu.edu.hk/as/tpginfoday. For details and application procedures of the programmes, please visit the website: www.polyu.edu.hk/study. As all applications received will be considered on a rolling basis, applicants are advised to submit their applications as early as possible.
