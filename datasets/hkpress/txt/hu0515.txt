[title]HKU to Hold Presentation Ceremony for Students Winning External Competitions (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]11 Apr 2005[/date]
Â 
HKU students who have excelled in competitions over the last year are to be rewarded for their efforts at a ceremony in their honour tomorrow (April 12, 2005).
More than one hundred students from various departments will be recognized of their excellence in their chosen fields of sports, architectural designs, business plans, computing, music, speech and debating contests, community services, etc.Certificates of commendation will be presented to the winning students at the ceremony, who have won awards in international competitions, regional competitions and local competitions during the period April 2004 to March 2005.
Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Hong Kong and Dr. Albert Chau, Dean of Student Affairs, will deliver addresses and present the certificates at the ceremony to be held at the Rayson Huang Theatre, the University of Hong Kong, at 6:00 p.m. on Tuesday, April 12, 2005.Reporters/photographers are cordially invited to attend the ceremony.
For media enquiries, please contact Miss Polo Leung at HKU's External Relations Office on 2859 2600.
