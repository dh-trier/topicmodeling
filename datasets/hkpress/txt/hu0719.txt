[title]HKU's Faculty of Engineering to Award Students and Honour Teachers[/title]
[date]04 Mar 2005[/date]
Â 
The Faculty of Engineering, the University of Hong Kong (HKU) will present prizes and awards to outstanding students and teachers in recognition of their achievements and excellence in research and teaching.
The Prize Presentation Ceremony will be held on Sunday, March 6, 2005 at 2:00 p.m. in Loke Yew Hall of the University's main campus.
Ir James Y C Kwan, JP, President of Hong Kong Institute of Engineers, will be the Guest of Honour and will present certificates to students who have won external prizes, including top places in international contests.T S Ng, Dean of Faculty of Engineering, will present certificates to faculty-based prize winners and students on Dean's Honours List.HKU's Vice-Chancellor, Professor Lap-Chee Tsui, will present certificates to recipients of the HKU Foundation Entrance Scholarship.
Professor Tsui will also present trophies to teachers who have made outstanding achievements in research and teaching.Some of them have won prestigious awards including membership of the Chinese Academy of Engineering and the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
Members of the media are cordially invited to attend.For enquiries, please contact Miss Vivian Li of the Faculty, Tel: 2241-5753.
