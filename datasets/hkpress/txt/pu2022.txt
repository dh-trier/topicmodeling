[title]Russian Universities Delegation at PolyU for a week-long training[/title]
A 15-member delegation from 13 Russian universities visited The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) from 13 to 17 December 2010 for a study programme on Higher Education Management in China organized by PolyU's Management and Executive Development Centre (MEDC).
The week-long programme provided the delegation with a better understanding of PolyU in its academic and research development and the various aspects of higher education in Hong Kong, China and the region.  
Mr Sidney Wong, Associate Director of the Institute of Enterprise, briefing the delegates on the development of Pearl River Delta into a key manufacturing base in China. Other topics covered in the study programme included Economic Development of Hong Kong, Latest Development of Higher Education in China and Hong Kong, and Strategic Planning of Programme Design for Higher Education.
