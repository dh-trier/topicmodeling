[title]PolyU to hold Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now inviting applications for its 2016/17 taught postgraduate programmes. To give prospective applicants first-hand information on its wide range of programmes, PolyU will hold the Info Day for Taught Postgraduate Programmes on 9 January 2016 (Saturday) from 2 p.m. to 6 p.m. on its campus.
Prospective applicants can get detailed information about the taught postgraduate programmes at more than 80 information seminars or programme consultation sessions organized by the six Faculties and two Schools of the University: Applied Science and Textiles, Business, Construction and Environment, Engineering, Health and Social Sciences, Humanities, as well as the School of Design and the School of Hotel and Tourism Management.
In the 2016/17 academic year, 94 taught postgraduate programmes will be on offer, including the following three new programmes:
MSc in Business Management
Executive Master in Digital Leadership
MSc in Disaster Management (Resilience, Response and Relief)
 The offer of the programme is subject to Senate's approval.
For more details of the Info Day, please refer to the Event Guide on web: www.polyu.edu.hk/as/tpginfoday. For details and application procedures of the programmes, please visit the website: www.polyu.edu.hk/study. As all applications received will be considered on a rolling basis, applicants are advised to submit their applications as early as possible.
