[title]PolyU presents MA Graduation Fashion Show at Hong Kong Fashion Week[/title]
With the support of the Hong Kong Trade Development Council, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)'s Institute of Textiles and Clothing (ITC) has staged its annual Master of Arts Graduation Fashion Show today (8 July) at the Hong Kong Fashion Week for Spring/Summer 2014. The Show is also one of the highlights of the Fashion Week.
During the Show, the creative works of 12 selected ITC's graduating students in the Master of Arts in Fashion and Textile Design programme are showcased. The students each presented a collection of six original outfits designed under their chosen themes, and demonstrated their innovation and creativity inspired by personal visions.
As the sole institute providing taught postgraduate programme in fashion and textiles in Hong Kong, ITC offers interesting and exciting opportunities to those who wish to pursue a career in the dynamic fashion and textiles industry. Since its inception in 1957, ITC has been committed to educational training and development for the advancement of Hong Kong's fashion and textile industry through various research and scholarly activities.
The Hong Kong Fashion Week for Spring/Summer 2014 is being held at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre from 8 to 11 July. PolyU ITC will also set up a booth to introduce its innovation 'i.Dummy' at the exhibition associated with this event.
