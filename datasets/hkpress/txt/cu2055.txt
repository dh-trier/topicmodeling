[date]19 July 2015[/date]
[title]CUHK Admits 305 Undergraduate Students from the Mainland[/title]
The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) has admitted, under the National Unified Universities and Colleges Entrance Examination System, 305 undergraduate students from 31 provinces, municipalities and autonomous regions of mainland China.  They are among the brightest students of their places of origin.  Apart from brilliant academic results, many of the students have excelled in extra-curricular activities and various national competitions.
