[title]PolyU invites applications to its taught postgraduate programmes[/title]
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is now accepting applications for 70 quality taught postgraduate programmes until 27 May 2004.
The taught postgraduate programmes of PolyU are practical and professionally-oriented to enhance the competitiveness of students to suit the needs of industry, business and the professions. All programmes are run by its six flagship faculties ranging from engineering to business administration, fashion and design, social work and various healthcare studies.
PolyU now offers six brand new self-financed postgraduate programmes which are in line with the market needs. They include: five part-time programmes ― comprising Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Applied Mathematics for Science and Technology (Decision Science), Master of Corporate Governance, Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Construction IT, Postgraduate Diploma in Chinese Language and Literature, and Postgraduate Diploma in English Language Studies ― and one full-time Master of Science/ Postgraduate Diploma in Applied Mathematics for Science and Technology (Actuarial &amp; Investment Science).
The public can obtain application details simply by visiting the University's Website at http://www.polyu.edu.hk/admission or one of the following ways:
