[title]Three New Student Halls at HKU to open this summer (Press Invitation)[/title]
[date]24 Jun 2005[/date]
Â 
A press briefing to announce the completion and names of the three new halls of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) will be held on next Monday (June 27).
Short presentations will be given by the three new wardens, followed by a sharing session by two former hall residents on how they have benefited from the hall education.
Dr Albert W L Chau, newly appointed Dean of Student Affairs, will also present an overview of future development plan of residential halls at the University.
Details of the press briefing are as follows:
Date: June 27, 2005 (Monday)
Time: 3:30pm
Venue: Room 218, Convocation Room, Main Building, HKU, Pokfulam, Hong Kong
Shuttle bus service will be provided for a preview of the new halls and their facilities after the press briefing, if weather permits.For media enquiries, please contact Miss Polo Leung at HKU's External Relations Office at 2859 2600.
