The Review Panel set up by the HKU Council to examine issues arising from the Centenary Ceremony on August 18, 2011 will hold its first meeting tomorrow (September 17).
Mr Lester Garson Huang, Convenor of the Panel will meet the media after the meeting on the ground floor of Knowles Building, HKU at about 12:15pm.
There will be no media arrangement for the meeting.
For media enquiries, please contact Mr K K Yuen, A-World Consulting Limited, Tel: 2114 2070 / 9677 8868, Email: kk.yuen@a-world.com.hk; or Ms Trinni Choy (Assistant Director (Media), Communications and Public Affairs Office) tel: 2859 2606 email: pychoy@hku.hk.
