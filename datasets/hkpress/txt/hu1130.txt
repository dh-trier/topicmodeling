HKU holds closing ceremony of Equal Opportunities Festival 2010

The closing ceremony of the Equal Opportunities Festival 2010 of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) was held today (March 31) at the global lounge of HKU.
Mr So Wa Wai, six-time Paralympic Gold Medalist; Commissioner for Labour Mrs Cherry Tse; and HKU Pro-Vice-Chancellor and Vice-President Professor Joseph Lee took part in the ceremony.
The HKU Equal Opportunities Festival 2010 festival has been held from March 9 to March 31 under the theme of cultural diversity.
