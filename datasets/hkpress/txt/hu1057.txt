The World University Service Hong Kong Branch (WUSHKUB) at the University of Hong Kong is to organise a public forum on "The Feasibility of the HKSAR Universal Suffrage In The Year 2007" on Thursday, October 9, 2003.Members of the press are cordially invited to attend.Details of the forum are as follows:
Date:October 9, 2003 (Thursday)
Time: 12:30pm - 2:00pm
Venue: Sun Yet-sen Plaza (outside the Main Library), the University of Hong Kong 
Guest Speakers: 
- Mr. Martin Lee
- Ms Audrey Eu
- Ms Ho Sau Lan
- Mr. Joseph Cheng Yu Shek
Moderator: Mr Robert Chau
For media enquiries, please contact Vicky Tang of WUSHKUB at 9834 2066.
