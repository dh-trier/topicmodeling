[title]PolyU experts share experience in active ageing promotion with Canadian delegation  [/title]
 
The Honourable Alice Wong, Minister of State (Seniors) of Canada (5th from left in picture), and her delegation visited The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) on 14 April 2013. The delegation was greeted by Professor Timothy Tong, President of PolyU, and members of the Institute of Active Ageing (IAA) including Professor George Woo, Emeritus Professor of PolyU’s School of Optometry, and Mrs. Teresa Tsien, Director of IAA. Information about the research and academic activities of the IAA, and the Project for the Third Age have been shared in the meeting.
