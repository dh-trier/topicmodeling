[title]PolyU President hosts Lunar New Year Luncheon for Development Foundation members[/title]
Professor Timothy W. Tong, President of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), hosted a Lunar New Year Luncheon at Bistro 1979, student training restaurant of the School of Hotel and Tourism Management, for members of the Development Foundation yesterday (16 February) in celebration of the Year of the Dragon.
Dr Yip Kit-chuen, Chairman of the PolyU Development Foundation, expressed his heartfelt thanks to all members of the Foundation for their ardent support to PolyU that is vital in contributing to its development in pursuit of academic excellence.
Before the luncheon, an annual general meeting was held during which members of the Foundation were briefed on the series of activities to be organised for the 75th anniversary of PolyU.
