[title]Visit of Swedish Rural Minister Eskil Erlandsson[/title]
Mr Eskil Erlandsson, Rural Minister of the Swedish Government, today (9 November) visited the School of Hotel and Tourism Management (SHTM) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).
The Minister met with SHTM students to do some Swedish cooking together at SHTM training restaurant Bistro 1979. He also witnessed the signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to strengthen academic exchanges and research collaboration between PolyU and Lund University.
The MoU was signed by Per Eriksson, President of Lund University, Sweden; who was represented by Senior Advisor Mr Bengt Streijffert in the visit and signing ceremony; and PolyU President Professor Timothy W. Tong.
The aim of Mr Erlandsson's visit is to promote Swedish forestry and food interests on the Chinese market. He will also meet representatives of the government in Hong Kong and business representatives, among others, and lead a trade delegation to visit China.
